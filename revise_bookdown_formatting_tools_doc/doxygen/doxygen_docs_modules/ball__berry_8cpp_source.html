27 * a * hs^2 + b * hs + c = 0
28 *
29 * This can be derived as follows:
30 * At steady-state
31 * E = gs * (hs - hi) -- Equation 1
32 * and
33 * E = gb * (ha - hs) -- Equation 2
34 *
35 * Substitute gs in equation 1 using the Ball-Berry model:
36 * gs = b1 * A * hs / cs + b0
37 *
38 * Where A is assimilation rate, hs is relative humidity at the surface of the leaf, and cs is the CO2 mole fraction at the surface of the leaf.
39 *
40 * Assume hi = 1 based on saturation of water vapor in the interal airspace of a leaf.
41 * Use the equality of equations 1 and 2 to solve for hs, and it's a quadratic with the coefficients given in the code.
42 */

44 const double b = beta0 + gbw - beta1 * acs; // Equivalent to b = beta0 + gbw - beta1 * assimilation / cs

51 } else {
